  • CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICY :

    Thank you for choosing DEVnDESK for your product and service needs. We are committed to delivering an exceptional experience to all our customers. However, we understand that situations may arise where you need to cancel your subscription or request a refund. Below is our policy for cancellations and refunds:

  • FREE DEMO/TRIAL :

    Before subscribing to any product plan or service, we offer a free demo or trial. If you wish to cancel your demo account or instance, please contact the appropriate representative or email us at contact@devndesk.com.

  • CONSULTATION AND SERVICES :

    We provide a standard consultation at no charge for service-related inquiries. Following this, we will present a proposal based on your needs. Once an agreement is made and an invoice is issued, you may request a cancellation through the assigned sales or marketing representative. Upon payment of the invoice, you will be assigned an Account Manager to assist you further.

  • REFUND AND CANCELLATION AFTER PAYMENT :

    Once a payment is processed, cancellations and refunds are generally not available. For products, we adhere to a zero-refund policy post-transaction. For services, we advise contacting your Account Manager to discuss any issues. Together, you will review the work completed and determine the next steps. Please note, personalized or custom services are non-refundable.

  • CANCELLATION AND REFUND REQUESTS :

    To request a cancellation or refund, please submit a written request through the specified customer service channels, such as email. Include all relevant details to help us process your request as efficiently as possible.

  • POLICY CHANGES :

    Please be aware that this cancellation and refund policy may be updated at any time, with or without prior notice. Any changes will be communicated via email or posted on our website. By continuing to use our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.
